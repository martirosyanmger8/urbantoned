Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the content of Urbantoned FitSolution after registration and payment?

You will be informed about all important matters related to your order via email with which you submitted your application. Also pay attention to the promotions/junk/spam folders, as the mail sometimes ends up there too. After submitting the application and completing the payment, you will receive a confirmation email within 3 working days confirming successful payment and confirmed participation in the Urbantoned FitSolution program. After that, you will receive a unique username and password via email, with which you will be able to access all content of the Urbantoned FitSolution program.

Can I train at any time or only at a specific time?

The workouts are recorded in advance and stay with you forever, so you can do/redo them whenever, wherever, and as often as you want. When you complete the entire program, you can do it again and make it even more challenging.

How long is the content available? Can I use the recipes and workouts forever?

All the content is available forever. It is important that you download the content within the allowed time frame (around 1 month). You will of course be reminded via email.

Can I apply and start the program later?

All content stays with you forever. It is only important that you make sure to download the content within the allowed time frame (around 1 month). You will of course be reminded via email.

Who is the Urbantoned FitSolution program suitable for? Is it suitable for both beginners and experienced individuals?

The Urbantoned FitSolution workout plan is suitable for both beginners and advanced athletes. At the beginning of each training session, I explain the exercises we will be performing as well as the correct execution of the exercises, and later, during the entire training session, I point out things that you need to pay special attention to.

How long are workout sessions in the Urbantoned program?

The training lasts 25-50 minutes, varying by workout and week. As the weeks progress, workouts get slightly longer. You'll also receive four daily recipes for breakfast, snacks, lunch, and dinner, totaling over 100 meal ideas in 28 days.

Does the menu consist only of classic ingredients bought in regular stores?

That's right. The recipes consist of ingredients that you can get in practically every store.

What is the approximate monthly cost for all ingredients?

It's hard to estimate monthly ingredient costs since it depends on what you already have at home. However, our meal plans are designed to be budget-friendly, and participants often find they save money with Urbantoned FitSolution's organized approach to meals.

Do the meal plans also contain nutritional supplements, such as proteins?

No. No recipe contains any additional nutritional supplements.

Where and how will we access workout plans and recipes?

After purchasing our program, you will receive your unique username and password via email, with which you will be able to access all the content from any device on the website. The website is designed very transparently, so that you will have all important information gathered in one place.

Are warm-up exercises and stretching included in the training?

They are. We warm up before each training, and cool down and stretch after the training.

Are the Urbantoned FitSolution workouts intended for both men and women?

Of course. Men and women both achieve exceptional results.

Is the program tailored to each individual?

The Urbantoned FitSolution program is not a classic one-month program in which you receive a demanding diet and strenuous workout plan, which quickly makes you give up. The goal of the Urbantoned FitSolution program is to teach you what it means to live a healthy lifestyle; to receive tons of new meal ideas; a lot of ideas for training and to realize that you can enjoy working out and sticking to a meal plan and still achieve your desired figure.

Are the workouts designed so that the entire workout is guided or are the exercises only explained?

The training sessions are fully guided. This means that you set up your phone/computer/tablet, press "play" and follow me and my instructions. Throughout the training, you also have a timer on the screen, so you don't even need a stopwatch.

Is this meal plan also suitable for those who work at night? Do I have to spend a lot of time preparing the meals on a daily basis?

Our meals are easy to make with no special cooking skills or ingredients needed. Many can be prepared in advance. You have lifetime access to Urbantoned FitSolution's content on the website, and the short, effective training sessions can be done at your convenience.

Can you follow the Urbantoned meal plans for one month or extend beyond?

The meal plans or recipes from the Urbantoned FitSolution program stay with you forever and you can use them for as long as you want. You can also change up the meals from the meal plans according to your needs so that they will not become monotonous despite prolonged use.

Is any equipment required for the workouts?

No equipment is required to complete the 28-day program at the beginner level. To carry out the 28-day program at an advanced level, it is advised you have a kettlebell, dumbbells and rubber band.

Is Urbantoned FitSolution suitable for all ages? What are the age limits?

The Urbantoned FitSolution program is suitable for all ages!

Is the Urbantoned program also suitable for vegetarians?

The Urbantoned FitSolution program is suitable for individuals who do not eat meat, as quite a few meals are vegetarian. In addition, with minor adjustments, you can also turn the other meals into meat-free ones.

Can you replace individual ingredients in some meals if you don't like them?

Of course! All meals at the Urbantoned FitSolution program are very flexible, so if you don't like a certain ingredient, it won't be a problem.

Is Urbantoned FitSolution suitable for muscle gain and body transformation, not just weight loss?

Of course! The Urbantoned FitSolution program is suitable for everyone, regardless of their goal, because you receive very detailed instructions that will teach you how to adapt the menu to your requirements. The most important aspect of this program is that you must not just stick to a certain menu, but finally understand why and how much you need to eat according to the goals you want to achieve.

Can you achieve results only by working out or sticking to the meal plan?

To achieve optimal results, it is best to combine both, but the results will be visible even if you only stick to the diet or do the workouts. What the result will be in this case depends on many factors.
